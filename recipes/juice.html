Healthy Green Juice

This classic shortbread cookie recipe makes rich, buttery, melt-in-your-mouth press cookies using just 4 simple ingredients that you probably have in your pantry. They're easy to decorate and perfect for Christmas cookie plates.

Ingredients

  • 4 stalks celery (leaves removed).
  • 2 green apples (halved).
  • 1 cucumber.
  • 6 leaves kale.
  • 1/2 lemon (peeled).
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Process celery, apples, cucumber, kale, lemon, and ginger through a juicer.
  3. Serve immediately or store in a glass jar in the fridge for up to one day; shake before drinking.
